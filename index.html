Our Story

Born from a Passion for Sri Lanka

TravelBooker was founded in 2020 as a product of CodeGen International — one of Sri Lanka's leading software companies. We saw a gap: Sri Lanka's extraordinary hotels and experiences were scattered across fragmented booking channels, making it difficult for travellers to discover and book the authentic island experiences they were seeking.

Our platform was built to bridge that gap — bringing together boutique villas, world-class resorts, eco-lodges, and heritage properties into a single, beautifully designed booking experience tailored specifically for Sri Lanka.

Today, TravelBooker serves direct travellers, travel agents managing group itineraries, and corporate partners booking employee travel — all through one unified platform.
